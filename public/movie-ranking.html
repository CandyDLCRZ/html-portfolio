The Best Netflix Series According to Katrin Erika

My top 3 favorite Netflix Series of all time.

Bridgerton

This series if very chic. I love the dresses, the jewelries and the hairstyles worn by the actresses. I also love how all the actors portrayed their respective roles. My favorite ofcourse is Daphne Bridgerton and her screen partner, The Duke of Hastings, Simon Basset.

The Crown

I love this series. It brings back so many memories about the British Royal Family. I am always fascinated about how the Monarchy works and on how King & Queens, Prince & Princesses actually live their lives. Although they say that The Crown is just a fiction and not based on real life, but watching this somehow gives you a glimpse of how living as a Royal would look like.

Suits

Now this one is different from the first 2 series I've mentioned above. It is an American Series about Corporate lawyers in Manhattan. I love this show because its really funny and the actors are very good in portraying their roles.
